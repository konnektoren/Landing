Learning Levels

Konnektoren offers a structured learning path from beginner to advanced levels, following the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR).

Level A1 - Beginner

Start your German language journey with fundamental concepts:

  • Articles (der, die, das)
  • Reflexive Pronouns
  • Personal Pronouns
  • Introduction to Konnektoren (Connectives)

Level A2 - Elementary

Build on your basics and expand your language skills:

  • Advanced Article Usage
  • Verb Conjugations
  • Perfect Tense with Pronouns
  • Noun Genders
  • Negation (Kein vs. Nicht)
  • Main and Subordinate Clauses
  • Verbs with Dative and Accusative
  • Konjunktiv II (Subjunctive)
  • Time-related Nouns
  • Case System (Nominativ, Akkusativ, Dativ)

Level B1 - Intermediate

Deepen your understanding with more complex grammar:

  • Past Tenses
  • Verbs with Prepositions
  • Interrogative Particles

Level C1 - Advanced

Master advanced concepts and nuances:

  • Advanced Konnektoren Usage
  • Complex Article Applications

FAQ

How do I start using Konnektoren?

Simply visit konnektoren.help and start exploring our interactive lessons and resources.

Is Konnektoren free to use?

We offer a variety of plans, including a free tier that gives you access to many of our features.

What is a verifiable credential badge?

A verifiable credential badge is a digital certificate of achievement that you can securely store and share. It's backed by web3 features, making it tamper-proof and easily verifiable by third parties.

How do I get started with SSI badges?

Once you complete certain challenges on Konnektoren, you'll be prompted to claim your badge. You'll need an SSI-compatible digital wallet to store your badges.
